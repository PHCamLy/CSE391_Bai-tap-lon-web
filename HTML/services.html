Our service

Our motto is to bring exquisite party spaces and events worthy of the brand value of the business. The extensive menu combines local specialties to deliver perfect dishes. 

WEDDING

This is a familiar place for many families and couples to hold weddings. Putan Hotel is a combination of modern and sophisticated space with the highlight of splendid crystal chandeliers and unique color layout that will bring pleasant surprises just for you.

RESTAURANT

At Putan restaurant, you are served buffet of European - Asian dishes in the morning from 6:00am - 10:00am. The menu is attractive, with many options for diners with more than 200 diverse dishes prepared with class, eye-catching, 4-star standards.

SPA

Get lost in the luxurious and charming Spa Putan Hotel oasis to enjoy moments of absolute relaxation in the midst of nature. Here, you can immerse yourself in unique spa experiences, distilled from the quintessence of Eastern and Western art.
